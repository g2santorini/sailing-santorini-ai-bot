### Perfect Answers

1. Ferretti 55 vs Ferretti 731

Both are excellent private yachts, but they offer a slightly different experience.

Ferretti 55 is more intimate and relaxed, ideal for smaller groups.
Ferretti 731 is larger, more spacious, and overall more premium.

If you prefer a higher-end luxury feel, 731 is the stronger choice.

2. Gems vs Diamond

Both are very high-quality experiences.

Gems is more balanced in comfort and value, with a relaxed atmosphere.
Diamond feels more premium, with more space and a cocktail included.

It really depends on whether you prefer value or a more upscale experience.

3. Red vs Platinum

Red is the best value option with a more lively atmosphere.
Platinum is much smaller and more relaxed, with fewer guests onboard.

If you prefer a quieter and more intimate experience, Platinum is the better fit.

4. Which cruise is more premium?

Diamond is the most premium shared cruise, with more space and additional onboard touches.

Platinum is also very refined, but more intimate.
For the highest level overall, Diamond is usually the top choice.

5. Which cruise is best for couples?

For couples, Platinum is the most intimate shared option.
Diamond is also a great choice if you prefer a slightly more premium feel.

For a special occasion, a private yacht offers the most exclusive experience.

6. Which cruise is best for a group of 6?

For 6 people, a private cruise is usually the best option for comfort and flexibility.

If you prefer a shared experience, Diamond is a strong premium choice.

It depends on whether you prefer privacy or a shared atmosphere.

7. Where do you stop for swimming?

Swimming stops usually include the Hot Springs, Red Beach, and White Beach.

Guests swim directly from the boat, as it does not dock at the beaches.

The exact stops may vary depending on weather conditions.

8. Do we swim at the Hot Springs?

Yes, swimming is done from the boat near the Hot Springs.

The boats do not dock, and swimming is optional.
Snorkeling equipment and noodles are provided onboard.

9. How much time do we have for swimming?

There are usually 2–3 swimming stops during the cruise.

Each stop lasts around 20–30 minutes.
Morning cruises typically allow slightly more swimming time.

10. Is the cruise crowded?

All cruises are designed to be comfortable and not overcrowded.

Platinum has the fewest guests, followed by Gems and Diamond.
Red is more lively, with a larger group.

11. We want fewer people onboard

For the most intimate experience, Platinum is the best option.

Gems and Diamond are also very comfortable with limited guests.
It depends on how private you want the experience to feel.

12. What food is included?

A freshly prepared BBQ meal is included onboard.

Vegetarian options are available, and some cruises also include a fish option.

13. What drinks are included?

All cruises include wine, soft drinks, and water.

Beer is included on selected cruises, and Diamond also includes one cocktail.

14. Are towels provided?

Towels are included on Gems, Platinum, Diamond, and all private cruises.

They are not included on the Red Cruise.

15. Can we go snorkeling?

Yes, snorkeling is available and equipment is provided free of charge.

Swimming is optional, so you can join only if you feel comfortable.

16. Is hotel pickup included?

Yes, round-trip transportation is included for most cruises.

If direct access is not possible, a nearby meeting point is arranged.

Pickup details are confirmed one day before.

17. From which port do you depart?

It depends on the cruise.

Diamond departs from Ammoudi in Oia.
Gems and Platinum operate one-way routes between Ammoudi and Vlychada.
Red departs from Ammoudi or Athinios.

18. How long is the cruise?

The cruise duration is usually around 5 hours.

Sunset cruises adjust their duration to include the sunset.
Morning cruises tend to allow more time for swimming.

19. Morning vs Sunset cruise

Morning cruises are more relaxed and focused on swimming.

Sunset cruises offer a more scenic and romantic experience, ending with the sunset.

It depends on the atmosphere you prefer.

20. We are 2 couples, what do you recommend?

For 2 couples, a private cruise offers the best experience.

If you prefer shared, Platinum is more intimate, while Diamond is more premium.

It depends on whether you prefer privacy or a shared premium option.

21. We want something special but not too expensive

Santorini Gems is usually the best balance between quality and price.

It offers a comfortable experience without going into premium pricing.

22. Can pregnant guests join?

In most cases, yes, but it depends on the stage of pregnancy.

For safety, we recommend checking with your doctor first.

23. Is the sea rough?

The caldera waters are usually calm.

Catamarans are very stable, so the experience is generally smooth.

24. What should we bring?

Swimwear, sunscreen, and a light jacket for the evening.

Shoes are removed onboard, so comfort is key.

25. Can we customize the itinerary?

Customization is possible on private cruises.

Shared cruises follow a set route, adjusted only for weather conditions
