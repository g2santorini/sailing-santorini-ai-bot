## Company Experience

We offer sailing cruises in Santorini to explore the unique beauty of the caldera.

Our cruises include:
- Scenic sailing along the caldera
- Stops at Hot Springs, Red Beach, and White Beach
- Swimming and snorkeling opportunities
- Onboard BBQ or meal experience
- Relaxed and comfortable atmosphere

Cruises are designed to combine sightseeing, swimming, and dining into a complete experience.

---

## Tours Overview

### Red Cruise
- Large group cruise (up to ~55 guests)
- More lively and social atmosphere
- Best value option

### Santorini Gems Cruise
- Mid-size group (up to ~20 guests)
- Balanced between comfort and value
- More relaxed than Red

### Platinum Cruise
- Smaller group (up to ~14 guests)
- More premium and quieter experience

### Diamond Cruise
- Premium small group (up to ~20 guests)
- Highest level shared cruise
- More space, comfort, and upgraded service

---

## Routes & Stops

Most cruises include:
- Hot Springs
- Red Beach area
- White Beach (Mesa Pigadia caves)
- Sailing past:
  - Aspronisi
  - Lighthouse
  - Black Mountain
  - Indian Face Rock

---

## Duration & Timing

- Morning cruises: usually around 09:30 departure
- Sunset cruises: usually around 14:30 departure
- Duration: approx. 5 hours

Sunset cruises are adjusted seasonally to ensure guests enjoy the sunset onboard.

---

## Food

- BBQ meal included (pork/chicken or vegetarian option)
- Vegetarian options available
- Some cruises may offer upgraded menu options

---

## Drinks per Cruise

- Red Cruise:
  Includes white wine, soft drinks, and water  
  ❗ Beer is NOT included

- Santorini Gems Cruise:
  Includes white wine, beers, soft drinks, and water

- Platinum Cruise:
  Includes white wine, beers, soft drinks, and water

- Diamond Cruise:
  Includes white wine, beers, soft drinks, water  
  + one cocktail per guest

---

## Drinks Rules (VERY IMPORTANT)

- Beer is NOT included on the Red Cruise
- Beer IS included on Gems, Platinum, and Diamond
- Never assume all cruises include the same drinks
- Always differentiate drinks per cruise

---

## Towels

- Not included on Red Cruise
