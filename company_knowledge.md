## Tours Overview

### Red Cruise
- Up to 55 guests
- Lively atmosphere
- Best value option
- BBQ meal included
- Drinks: wine, soft drinks, water
- No towels, no beer

### Santorini Gems
- Up to 20 guests
- Balanced comfort and value
- BBQ meal included
- Drinks: wine, soft drinks, water, beer
- Towels included

### Platinum Cruise
- Up to 14 guests
- More intimate and relaxed
- BBQ meal included
- Drinks: wine, soft drinks, water
- Towels included

### Diamond Cruise
- Up to 20 guests
- Premium shared experience
- BBQ meal included
- Drinks: wine, soft drinks, water, beer + 1 cocktail
- Towels included
- Ideal for Oia guests

---

## Diamond vs Platinum (Comparison)

Both cruises offer a high-quality small-group experience.

Diamond:
- Larger catamarans (Lagoon 560, Ipanema 58)
- Up to 20 guests
- More spacious layout
- Includes cocktail and fish option

Platinum:
- Smaller catamaran (Lagoon 450)
- Up to 14 guests
- More intimate setting

Guidance:
Both are excellent options.  
Choice depends on preferred space, atmosphere, and onboard experience.

## Swimming & Hot Springs

- The boat does not dock at the hot springs
- Guests swim from the catamaran

Important:
- Life vests are available for emergency use only
- Not recommended for swimming (restrict movement)
- Swimming noodles and snorkeling gear provided

Swimming is optional

---

## Transfers

- Round-trip transportation is included, except for no-transfer options
- Pickup usually takes place about 1 hour before departure, depending on the cruise and departure port
- For a 14:30 departure, pickup is usually around 13:30
- Exact pickup time and meeting point are confirmed one day before the cruise

### Hotel Pickup Coverage

- Pickup is available from most hotels and meeting points across Santorini
- If direct vehicle access to the hotel is not possible, a nearby pickup point may be arranged
- This is common in areas with limited vehicle access

## Sunset Experience

- Departure time is fixed (e.g. 14:30)
- Cruise duration adjusts based on sunset time
- Longer duration in summer, shorter in October

Important instruction:
If the user is concerned about missing the sunset → reassure clearly

All sunset cruises are designed so guests enjoy the sunset onboard.

## Policies

### Cancellation

Shared:
- Free up to 24h
- Less than 24h → no refund

Private:
- Free up to 72h
- Less than 72h → no refund

### Weather
Only cancelled by Port Authority  
Cloudy weather is not a valid reason

## Important Notes

- Boats do not dock on beaches
- Alcohol consumption is monitored
- Itinerary may change due to weather

## Seasonal Rules

- Morning cruises until 24 October
- 25 Oct – 15 Nov → sunset only
- 16 Nov – 15 Mar → no cruises

---

## Special Routes

- No cruises to distant islands (e.g. Mykonos)
- Private cruises may include nearby islands (Ios, Folegandros)
- No multi-day charters
